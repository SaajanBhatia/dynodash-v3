How to use the app

Registration

Upon registration, a user can either sign up as a restaurant account or a customer account.

Customer Registration

For a restaurant account, simply press the radio button and fill out the extra buttons.

Restaurant Registration

Upon Restaurant registration, the business owner is required to provide the business name, address and a description of what their business is.

Normal Sign In

This is what the normal sign in page looks like. Simply type your email and password, then sign in and you will be redirected accordingly.

QR Code Functionality

How does the QR code functionality work? The business owner downloads the barcodes for each table (found in the admin panel, described below). Then, the customer scans this QR code using their phone, in which it will directly take them to the menu page of that restaurant, storing their table number when they checkout.

Customer Account

Search your favorite restaurants here, then click on them. Have the option to check the restaurant on google maps.

Past restaurants are stored, so you don't always have to search up your restaurants. Pressing the map button will take you to google maps. Press the card to go to the restaurant menu page.

When pressing on the restaurant card, here is what the menu page looks like.

Users can select the quantity of an item they want, then press the floating checkout button in the corner of the screen. Pressing checkout will take you to checkout, which looks like this.

A table number is required. Upon checkout, a notification of the order is received.

Restaurant Account

A restaurant account can control the number of tables in a restaurant, view outgoing orders, download QR codes for each table and update their menu.

To update the tables, simply enter a number and press update.

The owner is also able to add new items to the menu, delete items, and update pre existing items such as their price, name or description.

The owner can view outgoing orders. Once the food is ready, they can complete the order and the customer will be notified.

From the admin dashboard, the business owner can download QR codes to the device, so that it can be printed and stuck to a table. Each table has a different QR code.

Files are stored to the download folder of the android device
